🔴 PU-98 — Credit or Recoveries

Negative budget entries representing credits, recoveries & deductions from gross expenditure

⚠️ Important: PU-98 represents credits and recoveries — these are negative entries that REDUCE gross expenditure. Budget (BG_ISL) = ₹ -1,242,885 '000s (= ₹ -124.29 Crore). This means ₹ 124.29 Crore is expected to be recovered/credited back during FY 2026-27. Actuals till date = ₹ -284,717 '000s (= ₹ -28.47 Crore) recovered so far. These are deducted from total gross revenue expenditure to arrive at net expenditure.
